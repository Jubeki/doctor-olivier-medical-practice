Coronavirus Advice

Boroughloch Medical Practice, 2020-05-18

The Scottish Government provides the following Coronavirus advice:

  • wear a face covering
  • avoid crowded places
  • clean hands and surfaces regularly
  • stay 2m away from other people
  • self-isolate and book a test if you have COVID-19 symptoms

All patients are requested to be aware of what you can and cannot do to suppress the virus by reading information from the Scottish Government.

Much useful information is also freely available from the NHS, which includes Test and Protect, the national scheme designed to stop the spread.
