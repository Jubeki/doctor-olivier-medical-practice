About Me

Dr. med. Corey B. Olivier is a young general practitioner situated in Bridlington, England. Within his visiting times and during appointments, he performs examinations on his patients, gives diagnosis, issues referrals and writes new prescriptions or repeats existing ones. In addition, appointments for vaccines can be made at our practice. Dr. Olivier is supported by a small team of staff members which handle most of the logistics regarding questions, appointments and patient data.
